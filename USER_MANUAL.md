# User Manual

This manual will guide you through the features of the E-Vaccination Appointment System.

## For Parents

### 1. Registration and Login

*   **Register:** To create a new account, go to the registration page and fill in your details.
*   **Login:** Once you have an account, you can log in using your email and password.

### 2. Managing Your Profile

*   After logging in, you can view and edit your profile information.

### 3. Adding and Managing Children

*   You can add your children's details to your account. This will help in booking appointments.

### 4. Finding Hospitals and Vaccines

*   You can browse a list of available hospitals and the vaccines they offer.

### 5. Booking an Appointment

*   Select a hospital and a vaccine, and then choose a convenient date and time for the appointment.

### 6. Viewing Appointments

*   You can view your upcoming and past appointments.

## For Hospitals

### 1. Registration and Login

*   **Register:** To register your hospital, go to the registration page and provide the required information.
*   **Login:** After registration, you can log in to your hospital account.

### 2. Managing Your Profile

*   You can update your hospital's profile information.

### 3. Managing Vaccine Inventory

*   You can add new vaccines to your inventory and update the stock levels.

### 4. Viewing Appointments

*   You can view the list of appointments booked at your hospital.
